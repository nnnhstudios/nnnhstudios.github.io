Privacy Policy for Sky Pulse

Last updated: [10/22/2025]

Welcome to Sky Pulse! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information:
We use Google Sign-In to authenticate users. This allows us to access basic profile information such as your username and email address.

Game Data:
We save your game progress (best scores, unlocked items, coins, and achievements) securely using Google Play Games cloud save. This helps you restore your progress on different devices.

Leaderboard Data:
Your high scores are submitted to Google Play Games leaderboards to display player rankings.

Advertising Data:
We use Google Ads to show advertisements in the game. Google may collect non-personal information including your Ad ID for personalized ads. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information through ads.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect or store any other personal data such as location, contacts, camera, or microphone access. No additional permissions are required.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Enabling sign-in and authentication via Google
  • Saving and restoring your game progress
  • Displaying leaderboards
  • Serving ads to support game development

We do NOT share your information with third parties beyond Google services used in the game.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data using Google Play Games infrastructure. However, no method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You can manage your Google account privacy settings at any time. If you have any questions regarding your data, please contact us at: nnnhstudios@gmail.com

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document.

For any more questions, please feel free to contact us.

Privacy Policy for Sky Pulse

Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to Sky Pulse. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information We Do NOT Collect:
We do not directly collect, store, or process personal sensitive data such as Name, Address, Phone Number, or Contacts.

b) Google Play Games Information:
We use Google Play Games Services for Sign-in, Leaderboards, and Cloud saves. Google may provide basic profile info like your public username.

c) Game Data:
Your progress (high scores, coins, achievements) is saved securely via Google Play Games cloud save.

d) Log Data & Device Information:
We and our partners collect device model, OS version, and crash reports solely for stability and troubleshooting.

e) Advertising Data:
We use AdMob, Unity Ads, and IronSource. They may collect Advertising ID (AAID) and approximate location to serve ads.

2. How Information Is Used

  • User authentication and game progress restoration
  • Displaying global leaderboards
  • Analyzing game stability (Crash logs)
  • Serving advertisements to support development
